Aquaculture: Feeding the World, Sustainably

The Big Three Aquaculture Experts is a private registered and owned entity established in 2021. It was registered within the laws of Zimbabwe in 2022 under registration number 6481/2022. Our team has vast experience across the aquaculture value chain in Zimbabwe and the region with an experience spanning over 20 years.The focus is anchored on sustainable aquaculture. We work across the country and region bringing knowledge that is supported by science, innovation while we strive to ensure that our sectorial solutions are context â€“specific in addressing both challenges and problems. We specialize in provision of services and production within the aquaculture sector including contract management trainings, development of Best Management Practices, design and building aquaculture infrastructure and various studies.

Enhance aquaculture profitability and productivity through working with all aquaculture value chain key actors, guiding them towards the best use of resources and provision to both customers and small-scale farmers a reliable market of high quality.

Mrs Privilage Masvavike

Director Administration and Technical Services

Privilage, a founding member of TBT, is the Director of Administration and Technical Services. With over 13 years of experience in aquaculture, she has worked with Lake Harvest Aquaculture for 9 years. During her tenure, she focused on tilapia rearing, fish health, disease management, and developing aquaculture standards for Zimbabwe, SADC, and East Africa. She chaired the company certification committee, developed best management practices, and collaborated with stakeholders to develop disease management protocols. She also planned business trials and analyzed data for decision-making.

Mr Sokonia Kaitano

Director Operations

Mr. Kaitano, a founding member of TBT and Director of Operations, has over 15 years of experience in Zimbabwe's aquaculture sector. He has designed and built infrastructure for ponds and cage farming, and has pioneered small and medium-scale cages in inland reservoirs. He has trained fish farmers on best management practices and is the Vice Chairperson of Zimbabwe Fish Producers Association and Chairperson of Development and Outreach under ZFPA.

Mr Abudala Napuru

Director Trainings and Commercial Services

Abudala Napuru, a former employee of Lake Harvest Aquaculture Zimbabwe, has joined TBT as Director Trainings and Commercial services. With over 15 years of experience in Sub Saharan Africa, he has worked with private sector and development partners on tilapia and catfish value chains. Napuru has conducted site suitability and feasibility studies, trained over 500 fish farmers, worked with small and large scale commercial fish farmers, conducted online trainings, designed training manuals, and participated in the Aquaculture Policy Gap discussion.
