Our Services

Unleash Aquatic Potential: Our Services

Production facility design

The company specializes in the construction and installation of low-cost cage models, HDPE cages, pond rehabilitation, hatchery and tank facilities, multipurpose access rafts, feed/accommodation badges, and aquanet designs, repairs, and installation.

Trainings and mentorship

We offer in-person and online trainings in the areas of aquaculture, biology of tilapia, environmental management, fish handling, health, illness, biosecurity, and economics. There are two categories for the trainings: Level 1 (non-management) and Level 2 (supervisory/management).

Technical Assistance

Hands-on assistance for new fish producers. We offer technical help to farmers during the first and second cycles, or until they are confident in managing their farm. supporting producers to raise productivity and improve efficiency in their individual systems, with a focus on commercial aquaculture solutions.

Community Engagement

Working with surrounding communities through needs analysis and utilizing sustainable livelihoods approach to measure, monitor and attain meaningful benefits

Consultancy

The study evaluates water quality and ecosystem fit for tilapia cultivation in water/dams, topography suitability for pond building, and water source suitability for survival. It also offers farmers with financial plans and business cases that outline the cash necessary to run the business over particular time periods.

All Things Aquaculture

Farmers have special demands related to their farm, such as space, purpose, and plans. We deal with each circumstance as it arises, as long as it is aquaculture-related.
