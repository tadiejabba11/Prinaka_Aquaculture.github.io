Prinaka Aquaculture Experts

Prinaka Aquaculture Experts Ltd, also known as The Big Three Aquaculture Experts, is a Zimbabwe-based private company with over 20 years of experience in sustainable aquaculture. They specialize in providing services and production, including contract management training, best management practices, and infrastructure design and construction.

Why Us?

We provides a unique approach to aquaculture value chain management, focusing on sustainability and science-based solutions. Our team of experts tailors solutions to each client's specific needs, ensuring the best possible results. Discover Our Aquatic Offerings

Community Engagement

The project involves collaborating with local communities to analyze their needs and implement a sustainable livelihoods approach to assess, track, and achieve substantial benefits.

Trainings and Mentorship

We provide both physical and virtual trainings on aquaculture, Tilapia biology, environmental management, fish handling, health, disease, biosecurity, and economics.

Construction, Assembling and Installation

The project involves the construction of affordable cage models, HDPE cages, pond renovations, hatchery and tank facilities, multipurpose access rafts, and aquanets.

Technical Assistance

We offer technical assistance to startup fish farmers, helping them increase yield and improve efficiency in their specific systems, focusing on commercial aquaculture strategies.

All Services Aquaculture

Farmers have unique needs based on their farm, space, purpose, and plans, and we address each scenario as aquaculture-related.

Consultancy Services

Site suitability studies assess water quality, ecosystem, terrain, and water sources for tilapia culture feasibility. Financial insite plans provide farmers with necessary funds for specific periods to run their business.
